😨 The Problem

Citizens often see development projects but lack clarity on:

  • 💰 Budget usage and allocation
  • 📅 Actual completion status
  • 🏠 Real civic impact on community
  • 📱 Timely project updates
  • 🔍 Transparency in decision-making

This leads to distrust and disengagement from civic processes.

✨ Our Solution

A geo-fencing based system that delivers:

  • 📍 Location-aware notifications
  • 📊 Real-time progress tracking
  • 💸 Complete budget transparency
  • 📱 Mobile-first engagement
  • 🔒 Privacy-first design

Empowering citizens with the information they deserve.
