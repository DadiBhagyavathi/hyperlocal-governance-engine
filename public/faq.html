Frequently Asked Questions

What is HyperGov? ▼

HyperGov is a geo-fencing based civic transparency platform that provides real-time information about public development projects in your area. We help citizens understand how public funds are being used and track project progress.

How does geo-fencing work? ▼

Our platform uses the Haversine formula to calculate distances between your location and public projects. When you enter a project's geo-fence area, you automatically receive relevant information about that project.

Is my location data tracked? ▼

No. We follow a privacy-first approach. Your location is only used temporarily to determine if you're near a project. We don't store, track, or sell your location data.

What information can I see about projects? ▼

You can view budget allocations, project timelines, completion status, responsible authorities, expected community impact, and real-time progress updates for all public development projects.

Is HyperGov free to use? ▼

Yes! HyperGov is completely free for citizens. Our mission is to make civic information accessible to everyone.

How do I report issues with a project? ▼

You can use our Feedback page to report issues, ask questions, or provide suggestions about any project. Your feedback is sent directly to the relevant authorities.

Which areas does HyperGov cover? ▼

We're continuously expanding our coverage. Currently, we focus on urban and suburban areas with active public development projects. Check the platform to see if your area is covered.

How often is project information updated? ▼

Project information is updated in real-time as we receive updates from government agencies and project managers. You'll always see the most current information available.

Can I receive notifications about specific projects? ▼

Yes! Once you create an account, you can customize your notification preferences to receive updates about projects you're interested in.

How can I contact support? ▼

You can reach us through the Feedback page or email us directly. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
